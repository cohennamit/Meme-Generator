This is a Meme Generator Program,
it was developed between 15-18/02/2023 as a "Sprint 2 Project" as a part of my studies in Coding Academy.


You start by choosing a Meme template to work on,
you can add unlimited lines of text and place them anywhere you want,
you can also remove any added line that is not one of the two meme template lines by clicking on it, and then clicking on the "Remove Line" button
If you wish to create a one line meme, you can select the second line and delete it's content.
You can change the lines font size and color using the buttons and you can choose one of three fonts available as the default one is the "Impact" font that's known for being a meme font.
You can locate the lines where ever you'd like via using the buttons or by dragging them around the meme image freely!
you can select a specific line by clicking on the "Switch Line" button or by simply clicking on it.
You can unselect a line in order to stop editing it or in order to remove the edit indicators (Important before saving/downloading/uploading) by clicking the "Unselect Line" button or by clicking outside of the line.
you can add one out of 5 selected emojis
you can download your creation, save it to your creations' gallery and even upload it to facebook for everyone to see!


Enjoy!
